Privacy Policy

How Stylique handles your information.

This application runs fully in the browser. Cart selections, theme preferences, and outfit builder choices are stored locally on your device using LocalStorage. No payment or server-side processing is performed.

Local device storage

Cart contents and preferences are stored in LocalStorage so your session feels continuous between visits.

Form submissions

Checkout and contact forms demonstrate UI behavior only. Submitted data is not transmitted to any backend in this demo.

Third-party assets

Photography is loaded from Unsplash — real editorial fashion imagery, never AI-generated placeholders.
